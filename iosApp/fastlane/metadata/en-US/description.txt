PennyWise is a private expense tracker and budget app that keeps your money — and your data — entirely on your device. No accounts. No cloud. No tracking. No ads.

Add expenses in seconds, import your bank statements, and watch a clean, categorized timeline of where your money goes — with budgets, trends, and insights that never leave your iPhone.

Why PennyWise
• 100% on-device & private — your data never leaves your phone
• No sign-up, no accounts — open the app and start
• Import statements — bring in Google Pay & PhonePe PDF statements
• Smart analytics — spending trends, category breakdowns, top merchants
• Budgets — set limits per category, with custom monthly cycles
• Subscription tracking — keep an eye on recurring payments
• Smart rules — auto-categorize your transactions
• Multi-currency support
• Beautiful dark mode
• Open source (AGPL-3.0)

Expense tracking, your way
Add income and expenses fast, or import a statement to backfill your history. Every transaction lands in a searchable, categorized timeline — a spending tracker that's always up to date.

Budgets that fit real life
Set category budgets and track progress at a glance. Custom cycle start days mean your "month" can begin on payday, not the 1st.

Private by design
All processing happens on your device — no servers, no API calls, nothing leaving your phone. There are no accounts to create, and the full source is open (AGPL-3.0) so anyone can verify exactly what the app does.

Free, with optional Pro
PennyWise is free to use. An optional Pro upgrade unlocks power-user extras — unlimited custom rules, CSV export, and more — and directly supports a solo developer building PennyWise independently.
